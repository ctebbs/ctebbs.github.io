ABOUT ME

I'm a recent graduate from University of Washington, Bothell with a Bachelor of Arts in Interactive Media Design, and a minor in Computer Science and Software Engineering. I'm currently working as a Research Associate at Pacific Northwest National Laboratory, where I'm combining my tech and design skills to work through complex issues in various fields. I've developed both programming and design skills working on Virtual Reality, mobile, and web applications.

In my free time I enjoy drawing, playing video games, and learning more about design. I also produce artwork and storylines for some tabletop RPG groups. My hope is that one day the designs I create will be able to tell a story or provide meaningful critical insights.

I'm always looking for new projects and ways to learn more about design tools. Check back often to see growth as I continue my journey.
