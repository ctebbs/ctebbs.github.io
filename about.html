ABOUT ME

I'm a senior currently attending University of Washington, Bothell. I'm studying Interactive Media Design, and am interested especially in mobile application development and UX/UI design . I have a strong passion for art and technology, and am focusing on Computer Science for a minor.

In my free time I enjoy drawing, playing video games, and learning more about design. I also produce artwork and storylines for some tabletop RPG groups. My hope is that one day the designs I create will be able to tell a story or provide meaningful critical insights.

I'm always looking for new projects and ways to learn more about design tools. Check back often to see growth as I continue my journey.
